Presents What to give?

Don't worry about the gift! Your presence is enough for us. However, if you really insist...
We already have everything needed for our household like utensils, furniture, appliances, etc. Therefore, we will be grateful if your gift doesn’t consist any of those.
As for our interests, we like board games, travelling, creating memories through various experiences - both extreme and not so :).
You could always just give us a congratulation card ... with some money … or maybe just money … or maybe just a card! It’s up to you! No matter what it is we will appreciate it! We hope that we have been helpful. :)

Dress code What to wear?

The question that bothers every lady out there!
We will be outdoors, so make sure you bring an overcoat in case it gets cold.
Other than that, wear something you feel comfortable in and something that brings your inner beauty out for everyone to see! :)

Transport I don't have a car! What should I do?

The last section of the invitation is a confirmation form where you can let us know if you will need transportation to the location or not. From there on we will contact you to help with the arrangement.
