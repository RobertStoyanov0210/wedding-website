Presents What to give?

Don't worry about gifts! Your coming is enough for us. But if you really insist...
We already have everything needed for a home, such as utensils, furniture, appliances. We will be very grateful if you don't overwhelm us with more.
We like board games, any kind of different experiences (extreme or not :)) and to travel. Of course, you can only give us a gift card with money ... or without the gift card ... or the money. You decide! Good luck! We hope we have been helpful. :)
